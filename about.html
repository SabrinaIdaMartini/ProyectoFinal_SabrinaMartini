about me

I love learning new things!

I first started out as a fashion designer, something I’ve been interested in since I was a teenager. During this career I discovered that I really liked graphic design so once I finished fashion school I went to graphic design school. It was a great experience because it introduced me to UX design and web development. This is my current (most challenging) hobby but I’m not going to give up until I reach a professional level and hopefully work as a developer someday.
